Here are the funtionality amended:

1. Choose columns to display in meta, content or title
2. side bar panel on right hand side like TOC
   > You can select which column as TOC in setting panel
3. change color and opacity
4. change size of title part and content part
   > See the effect of size change by setting zero first turn adjust it
5. enable to collapse all chat thread content and title partially, with meta hidden.
   > Reveal upon Clicking
6. Text filter with or without regex
7. Date filter accept format 2023-10-06 or 20231006
8. Date filter got the reset button applies to reset All filter
